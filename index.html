About Me

I am a scientist, data scientist, and multidisciplinary team leader. My style is shaped by over 20 years of professional experience, scientific curiosity, and empathy.

Cross-Sector Experience

My experience across academia, government, and industry gives me insights into the drivers, challenges, and operating models of each sector. I am deeply motivated by working with people from diverse backgrounds. My best ideas have come from purposeful conversations, built on personal connections and trust.

Remote Sensing

I primarily work with multispectral optical sensors due to their widespread availability, but I'm constantly exploring new technologies driven by curiosity and application needs. To construct the best model of Earth, we need measurements from as many perspectives as possible. The development of the first analysis-ready synthetic aperture radar dataset and exploration of hyperspectral data for landscape characterisation are just some examples of my work.

Turning Data into Insights

The value of data lies in the insights it reveals. My professional career started with surveying the sky, studying powerful cosmic explosions to understand the history of the universe. Today, I analyse Earth observation data to map natural resources and track environmental changes. I take a purposeful approach, starting with the real question and balancing accuracy with practicality.
